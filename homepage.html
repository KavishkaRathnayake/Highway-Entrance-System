Easy Highway Ticketing

Welcome To Sri Lanka Highway Entrance

An Automated Highway Ticketing System based solution that issues traffic tickets without human intervention. Using cameras, sensors, and software, it monitors vehicles on the road for violations such as speeding, running red lights, or illegal lane changes. When a violation is detected, the system records the details—like the vehicle’s speed, license plate number, and time of the incident—and automatically sends a ticket to the driver. This reduces the need for law enforcement officers to manually stop and ticket drivers, making the process faster and more efficient.
